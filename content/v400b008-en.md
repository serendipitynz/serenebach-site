Serene Bach v4.0.0-beta.8 has been released.

* Bug Fix: The AI compose screen (`/admin/ai/compose`) now respects the display locale configured for the admin panel.
* Development: Introduced golangci-lint and gradually enabled multiple linters to clean up the codebase.
* Development: Split many high-complexity functions to improve readability and maintainability (gocyclo / gocognit / gocritic).
