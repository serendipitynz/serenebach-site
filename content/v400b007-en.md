Serene Bach v4.0.0-beta.7 has been released.

* New Feature: Added pinned entries, which float selected posts to the top of the home and category pages.
* New Feature: Comment acceptance can now be toggled per entry.
* Improvement: Improved the custom tag admin UX with a dynamic hint and a click-to-copy list.
* Bug Fix: Fixed the layout and save behavior of the pinned-entry checkbox in the entry editor.

Download: [Serene Bach v4.0.0-beta.7](https://github.com/serendipitynz/serenebach/releases/tag/v4.0.0-beta.7)
