Serene Bach v4.0.0-beta.21 has been released.

* Fix: Hardened the full-text search (FTS5) startup probe and Open Graph card regeneration.
* Fix: Preserved the correct parent-child relationship when importing from SB2 / SB3 even when the parent is category 0.
* Docs: Added a non-technical migration guide for moving from SB2.
* Development: Strengthened tests for configuration, character encoding, boundary conditions, and SB3 template compatibility, and tidied up the developer documentation and contribution conventions.

Download: [Serene Bach v4.0.0-beta.21](https://github.com/serendipitynz/serenebach/releases/tag/v4.0.0-beta.21)
